Cybersecurity Measures and E-Business Ethics

Amazon has also adopted various cybersecurity measures such as the use of an encrypted version of the SSL, and the two-factor authentication (2FA) when making secure payments (Krebs on Security, 2015). Periodic security audits detect and mitigate possible vulnerabilities. Amazon also adheres to the provisions of GDPR, which guarantees the openness of data use. To improve cybersecurity, Amazon could integrate AI-powered fraud detection systems to proactively detect suspicious activity. Ethical conduct in the company is reflected in its adherence to data privacy and consumer rights, and sustainability and ethical sourcing practices still can be enhanced to remain ahead of the digital economy.
