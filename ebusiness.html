E-Business Model of Amazon

Amazon has an e-business model known as Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) model, which enables it to sell directly to consumers via its web and mobile applications (Doshi and O’Neal, 2024). This cuts off the intermediaries and Amazon controls pricing, marketing, and customer interactions. Among the key advantages of this model, the increase in profit margins is observed since no commissions to the retailers. The international network of company logistics guarantees quick and secure delivery, which increases customer satisfaction. But, there are issues such as high customer acquisition costs and large inventories and customer data. Nonetheless, the D2C model is a key to the ongoing innovation and expansion of Amazon in different industries, such as retail and cloud services
