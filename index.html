Amazon's Brief History

  • Founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994.
  • Began as an online bookstore.
  • Expanded to global e-commerce, cloud computing, and digital services.
  • Now one of the largest e-commerce companies globally.

Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) Model

Amazon uses the Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) model, eliminating intermediaries and offering personalized services directly to the customer. This model enhances Amazon's profit margins and enables better control over customer relations and pricing.
