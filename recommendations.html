Recommendations

In order to enhance the digital business strategy of Amazon, there are a number of key measures that ought to be taken. To begin with, it is important to be able to optimize the mobile experience, as the number of mobile users increases. Quick and easy navigation will be a part of the user experience and customer satisfaction. Moreover, to better interact with customers, it is important to improve data analytics capabilities to enhance personalized marketing and fine-tuning customer targeting (Theodorakopoulos and Theodoropoulou, 2024). The other notable suggestion is to invest in interactive social media campaigns, which will not only increase brand awareness but also customer loyalty owing to increased engagement. Moreover, the deployment of AI-driven fraud detection will considerably increase cybersecurity and ensure the protection of customer information better and make the Amazon platform more trusted. Finally, the prioritizing of SEO optimization and use of organic traffic will enable Amazon to decrease the use of paid advertisements, generating long-term sustainable growth. With such suggestions, Amazon will be able to expand its online presence, customer interaction, and remain at the forefront of the e-commerce sector.
