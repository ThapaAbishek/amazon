Competitive Strategy of Amazon

Amazon is using a differentiation strategy where it offers a variety of products, speedy shipping, tailored shopping and services such as Amazon Prime and AWS. One of the main aspects of the competitive advantage of the company is its customer-focused approach where it invests in technology to enhance shopping experiences. Predicting consumer behaviour and offering personalised recommendations is done using data analytics and AI, which lead to repeat business (Nugroho, 2025). The low-cost strategy of Amazon allows dominance in the market, and brand-loyalty programs guarantee its long-term retention. Amazon leads the pack even though other e-commerce giants are competing with it because of its emphasis on innovations
