Digital Business Strategies

The digital business strategy used by Amazon takes advantage of technology to improve the customer experience and simplify operations. It has hugely invested in cloud computing (AWS), which provides scalable, secure and flexible solutions to both large and small businesses. The online shopping site has been streamlined in terms of user experience (UX), which includes customized options, one-click buying, and quick shipping under the Amazon Prime (Paporisharma, 2022). One of its digital strategies is data analytics and machine learning, which allow Amazon to forecast consumer behavior, change the price, and enhance product recommendations. Nevertheless, issues like optimization of mobile and sustenance in supply chain management must be considered to continue growth
