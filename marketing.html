Digital Marketing Strategies

Amazon has adopted the multi-channel strategy of digital marketing comprising of social media, search engine optimization, affiliate marketing, and paid advertising. SEO will help to make the product visible on the search engine, whereas the paid advertisements will be offered to the potential customers on Google and Facebook (Flying V Group, 2023). Affiliate marketing is a method by which other websites and influencers can promote traffic to Amazon. Another aspect of influencer marketing is its ability to reach larger audiences. Amazon aims at customers with personalized services, which are its strength as the customer information is obtained to provide personalized advice, emails, and advertisements. This coordinated marketing strategy will make Amazon visible and pertinent to its customers, which makes it dominate the e-commerce market
