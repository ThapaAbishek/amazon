E-CRM and Social Media Channels

The e-CRM system of Amazon is instrumental in ensuring a good customer relationship. Amazon tracks customer preferences and purchase history, sending personalized recommendations, which makes the shopping experience better (Argoid Analytics, 2026). The use of social media such as Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter to communicate with customers and advertise products. Amazon also engages these platforms in customer care, responding to questions in a timely manner. As an additional way to enhance the interaction, Amazon ought to emphasize on interactive content and user-created reviews, which would help build the trust in the brand and customer loyalty (Mudambi and Schuff, 2010). Rich CRM software might also allow real-time support, which enhances more customer satisfaction.
