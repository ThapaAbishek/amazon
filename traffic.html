Customer Traffic Analysis

Amazon had 2.312 million visits in January 2026, which is a 30.5% decrease compared to the last month (Lee, 2026). The number of visits reached 68.96% via mobile devices and 31.04% via desktops. The direct traffic was 36.63, organic search was 29.15, and paid traffic was 21.76 (Buy Box Experts, 2025). The social media channels (Instagram, Pinterest and YouTube) contributed 8.93 per cent of the total traffic. These lessons indicate that Amazon should be mobile for its development. To enhance the level of engagement and organic traffic, the company needs to concentrate on the issue of mobile navigation and invest additional funds in social media campaigns.
